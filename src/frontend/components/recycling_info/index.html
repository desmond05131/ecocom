Klang Valley Recycling

by HuiYing Yap

    The Klang Valley Recycling program offers curbside pickup every Tuesday and provides separate bins for paper, plastics, and metals. Residents are encouraged to rinse their recyclables and place them out by 7 a.m. Participation in this program has already reduced landfill waste by 30% in the last year alone, proving that small efforts can lead to big results.

Venue & Time

    Jalan Teknologi 5, Taman Teknologi Malaysia, Kuala Lumpur

    Every Wednesday, 7.00 a.m. - 9.00 a.m.

What You Can Recycle
  • Paper & cardboard
  • Plastic bottles & containers
  • Glass
  • Metal cans
  • Electronics (e-waste)
