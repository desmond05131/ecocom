Gardening in Pavilion

by Darren Tong

Venue & Time

    Jalan Teknologi 5, Taman Teknologi Malaysia, Kuala Lumpur

    Every Wednesday, 7.00 a.m. - 9.00 a.m.

Description

    Join our vibrant community garden where neighbors come together to grow fresh, organic produce while sharing knowledge, tools, and friendship. Whether you're an experienced gardener or just starting out, there’s a plot for everyone. Let’s cultivate not only plants but also a greener, healthier community.
